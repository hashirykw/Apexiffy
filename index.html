Questions

Everything you'd ask before joining

Are my progress photos private?+

Yes — photos are private by default. Only you (and your coach, if you're Elite) can view them. You choose if a transformation ever goes public.

Do I need gym equipment?+

No. The guide generator adapts — tell it home, dumbbell-only, or full gym, and the exercises adjust to what you have.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Anytime, one tap, no questions. Your data and photos remain in your account even on the free plan.

How do payments work in Pakistan?+

JazzCash, Easypaisa, and all major debit/credit cards. Annual plans get two months free.

What makes this different from a PDF plan?+

A PDF never changes. Apexiffy recalculates your calories, adjusts your split, and grades your week — every week, from your real logs.
